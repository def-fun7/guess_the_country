How to Play

  • Click on Shuffle and the name of the Country you are supposed to find will appear in place of "Let's Start".
  • Click on the feature in the below map, which you think is the right answer.
  • A popup will appear where you clicked.
  • If you are sure this is the right answer, then click "Choose".
  • If your answer was correct or wrong then, a text line will appear accordingly on top of the map as well as a gif will play in the map frame for couple of seconds.
  • Please, let the gif end before retrying.
  • Clicking on "Go back" will zoom out the map to intial zoom level (1) and set (0, 0) as center.
  • If your answer was right, the win counter will go one number up, else the lose counter.
  • You can try for a country as many times as you like, however, it could cause site to halt.
  • Shuffling without choosing any answer, will cause the skip counter to count and will not be consider as a lose point.
  • You tries can be seen in the History on top right corner.
  • Reset button or refreshing the page will clear everything, including score. The webpage doesn't store any kind of personal information from you.
  • For more Information, check README
